About The Company

Surge is an educational organisation based in Mumbai. We believe that world needs innovators more than anything else and at Surge, our mission is to empower every student by providing them right knowledge, training them with latest cutting-edge technology and help them to innovate or work with the existing tech innovators/organization. We Provide Industry training and Recruitment in the field of Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Android, Full Stack Development and many more. We have trained students from more than 20 Engineering Colleges and corporates. All our alumni are working with prestigious companies like Reliance Jio, Disperze, Accenture, Deutsche bank, IIT- Bombay and many more with the highest package of 18 LPA
